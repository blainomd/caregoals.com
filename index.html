Advance Care Planning

The conversation that changes everything

Most families never have this conversation. Not because they don't care -- because they don't know where to start. Sage starts it for you.

Inspired by Five Wishes, the most widely used advance directive in America, Sage walks your family through five questions that matter:

1. Who speaks for me when I can't?

Your healthcare proxy -- the person who knows your heart.

2. What kind of medical treatment do I want?

Life support, resuscitation, ventilators -- your preferences, documented clearly.

3. How comfortable do I want to be?

Pain management, dignity, surroundings -- the details that define quality of life.

4. How do I want people to treat me?

Bathing, grooming, music, prayers, visitors -- what presence looks like.

5. What do I want my loved ones to know?

Forgiveness, gratitude, final messages -- the words that outlive us.

Sage guides the conversation. You talk. It listens. At the end, you get a complete advance directive -- generated from your own words, not a form.

Medicare covers advance care planning conversations (CPT 99497/99498). Your physician can bill for the time.
