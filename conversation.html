The conversations worth having

Five domains. One at a time. Sage guides your family through each one.

Domain 1

What matters most

Before any medical question, Sage asks about values. What does a good day look like? What brings joy? What would make life feel not worth living? These answers shape everything else in the care plan.

Most advance care planning tools jump straight to CPR preferences. CareGoals starts with who you are — because medical decisions are downstream of that.

Sample questions
"What does a good day look like for you right now?"
"What activities or relationships are most important to your quality of life?"
"What are you most afraid of losing as you get older?"
medical_services
Domain 2

Medical decisions

CPR, hospitalization, life support, tube feeding — doctors need these answered in a crisis. CareGoals helps you think through each one in a calm setting, not an ICU. Sage explains each option in plain language and captures nuance — not just yes/no boxes.

Sample questions
"If your heart stopped, would you want doctors to try to restart it?"
"How long would you want a breathing machine used if you couldn't breathe on your own?"
"What matters more to you — length of life or quality of life?"
home
Domain 3

Living and care preferences

Where do you want to live as you age? Who do you want helping you? What daily routines matter? These practical preferences are often more important than medical ones — and rarely documented.

Sample questions
"Would you want to stay home even if it required significant support, or would a care facility be acceptable?"
"Are there specific people you'd want — or not want — involved in your daily care?"
gavel
Domain 4

Who decides

If you can't speak for yourself, who speaks for you? CareGoals helps you designate a healthcare proxy, give them specific guidance, and make sure they know and accept the role — before any crisis.

Sample questions
"Who do you trust most to make hard decisions on your behalf?"
"What would you want that person to know before they ever had to use this?"
auto_stories
Domain 5

Legacy and meaning

What do you want people to remember about you? Are there stories your family should know? Is there something you want to say to the people you love? CareGoals gives you a place to capture it — and share it.

Sample questions
"What's something about your life your family might not fully know or appreciate?"
"If you could say one thing to each person you love, what would it be?"
