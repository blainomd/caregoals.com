What Sage asks in the first session

1
Who is this plan for?

Sage starts by understanding whether this is for you or a family member, and who else might be involved in the conversation.

"Are you doing this for yourself, or are you planning on behalf of a parent or loved one?"
2
What does a good day look like?

Before any medical questions, Sage asks about what makes life meaningful. This anchors everything that follows.

"Tell me about what a really good day looks like for you right now — the things that matter."
3
What worries you most?

Understanding fears helps Sage frame the care plan around real concerns — not generic checklist items.

"What do you worry most about as you think about getting older or needing more help?"
4
Where would you want to be?

Home, a facility, near family — these preferences are highly personal and often undiscussed until crisis.

"If you needed help with daily activities, where would you most want to be living?"
5
Who do you trust?

This conversation leads to healthcare proxy designation — the single most important document in a crisis.

"If you couldn't speak for yourself, who do you trust most to make medical decisions on your behalf?"

Frequently asked questions

Is this a legal document?

Your first-session care summary is not a legal advance directive — but it informs one. Co-op.care members can generate state-specific advance directives from their CareGoals summary. The summary itself is valuable on its own as a guide for family and caregivers.

Does my doctor see this?

Only if you share it. CareGoals never automatically sends your information to anyone. You generate a shareable link and decide who receives it.

What if my parent refuses to talk about it?

You can do a session yourself first — what you would want for them based on conversations you've had. Then share it with them as a starting point. Many families find it easier to react to something already written than to start a conversation from scratch.

How long does it take?

First session: 20–30 minutes. Updates: 5–10 minutes. There's no deadline and nothing expires — Sage will be here whenever you're ready.

Is my information private?

Yes. CareGoals is HIPAA-compliant. Your data is encrypted end-to-end. We never sell your information or share it with advertisers.

Can I do this without my parent present?

Yes. You can do a session for your own advance care planning, or you can do a session capturing what you know about a parent's wishes — and then share it with them for corrections and additions.
